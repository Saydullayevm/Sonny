Platform Features

Built for the Road. Designed for Compliance.

Every feature in the Sonny ELD platform exists to keep your operation efficient, audit-ready, and fully compliant — without adding complexity to your day.

01

Electronic Hours of Service Logging

Sonny ELD automatically captures and records your driving hours directly from the truck's engine, eliminating manual logbooks and reducing human error. Every duty status change is timestamped and stored in real time, giving both drivers and fleet managers an accurate, always-up-to-date record of HOS compliance.

  • Automatic duty status detection
  • Instant log editing with required annotations
  • Exempt and short-haul support
  • Co-driver and team driving modes
02

Real-Time GPS Fleet Tracking

Monitor every vehicle in your fleet from a single intuitive portal. Live location updates, route history, and geofence alerts give you total visibility — whether you manage one truck or one hundred.

  • Live map with 4G/LTE updates
  • Full trip history & route replay
  • Geofence alerts & stop detection
03

IFTA Mileage & Fuel Reporting

Automated per-state mileage tracking means quarterly IFTA filing takes minutes, not hours. All data is collected automatically during every trip and compiled into a ready-to-submit report.

  • Per-state mileage breakdown
  • Fuel purchase tracking
  • Audit-ready report export
04

Violation Alerts & Compliance Monitoring

Proactive alerts notify drivers and fleet managers of potential HOS violations before they occur. Stay ahead of audits and DOT inspections with a continuously monitored compliance dashboard.

  • Pre-violation warnings
  • Real-time compliance dashboard
  • DOT inspection mode
05

Digital DVIR Inspections

Replace paper-based pre- and post-trip inspection reports with a fast, paperless digital process. Drivers complete inspections in the platform, and fleet managers receive instant notifications for any defects reported.

  • Pre- and post-trip reports
  • Defect flagging & repair tracking
  • Permanent inspection history log
06

Engine Diagnostics & Fault Codes

Receive real-time engine fault code alerts directly through the platform. Identify mechanical issues early, reduce unplanned downtime, and maintain a detailed maintenance history for every vehicle in your fleet.

  • Real-time fault code detection
  • Maintenance scheduling reminders
  • Vehicle health history
